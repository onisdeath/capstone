Best Movies I've watched

Some of the movies that I've enjoyed watching.

Interstellar

I had the oppurtunity of seeing it at the cinema when it first came out and I was just amazed and so baffled by the beauty of this film.

Big Hero 6

One of my favorite childhood movies, the characters, the story, and even though it may be controversial, the villian was executed so nicely, still makes me teary-eyed sometimes.

Fast and Furious:Tokyo Drift

Even though the film "kills off" one of my favorite characters in the franchise, I still adore it for the fact that it got me into JDM cars and in general cars.

Anna Karenina

While the movie itself is pretty different from the book, I still adore it for the actor and actress', Aaron-Taylor Johnson and Keira Knightley, performances.
